About Janu

Originally from Cambridge, MA, I've spent most of my life running up and down the East Coast eating bagels and drinking coffee. I studied at Wesleyan University leaving with a Bachelors in Arts & Sciences in Biological Sciences. Afterwards I received a Certificate of Design in Architectural Design from the University of Washington. For the past year I've been studying software engineering and web design, and finished a Full-Stack program in October 2020 with the Flatiron School.

Since arriving to Seattle, WA I've explored the natural splendor of the region and fell in with a really beautiful community of dancers and artists around the city as well as find my way into the startup world.

How I got into coding

Time after time, at work - in life - I saw the need for solutions that didn't exist, to problems that impacted many. It didn't make sense to me that these solutions weren't being made or implemented. Without realizing it, I reflexively sought ought ways to change that.

It drove me to learn about code, and after a few months of self-study I decided to enroll in a course. Some months later, and now I can do things I never thought I'd think to do. The kinds of things I wanted to create seemed to get closer. The process of being able to bring an idea to life became rewarding in a way that was almost intoxicating - it was progress. It's still challenging, but I don't see any reason to stop.

Why I Code

I've learned that my love for design and creating, as well as my passion for helping those around me can be found within building applications. That and the fact that the field is constantly evolving, pushing boundaries and innately innovative. This appeals to me, because I love learning new things to make and theres so much to learn - and so much to make. I just hope the things I make are things that do something people can appreciate.

Code Interests

My future goals include learning more about IoT, sustainability, smart-buildings, vertical farming, smart-health, tele-health, cloud-computing, AI, cyber-security and digital entertainment/media.

If you have any leads or would like to chat more, send me a message!
